About Us

Welcome to Pixel Panic Games, where we craft byte-sized games packed with big personality.

We're an indie studio focused on creating experiences that are fun, distinctive, and full of character. From fast-paced arcade challenges to quirky, precision-based adventures, our games are designed to delight, challenge, and entertain players of all kinds.

At Pixel Panic Games, we believe great ideas don't need to be big—they just need to be bold. Every project we create is driven by creativity, experimentation, and a love for playful design.

Small studio. Big ideas. Endless pixels.
