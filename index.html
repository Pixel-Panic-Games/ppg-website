Jet Pack Kiwi

Jet Pack Kiwi puts a bird in a jetpack, tasked with flying through obstacles made of trees and hanging plants. Each successful gap increases your score, and the challenge grows gradually with tighter gaps, clusters, and varying heights.

Four modes provide subtle changes in atmosphere — from cloudy daytime to dusk, neon-lit late night vibes, and night — giving each flight a slightly different feel.

Timing and precision are key. Each run offers a measured challenge, with progress that encourages careful play.

Jet Pack Kiwi is a simple, focused arcade experience. Flights, gaps, and small adjustments — that's the rhythm of the game.
